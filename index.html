"Shootout at Wadala" is a 2013 Indian crime film. based on the book
"Dongri to Dubai" by Hussain Zaidi. It is a dramatized depiction of the
,1 st ever registered encounter by the Mumbai Police, which took place in 1982.



         ( 1944 - 1982 )
