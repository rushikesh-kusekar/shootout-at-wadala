' ISAQUE BAGWAN '



Isaque Bagwan

Birth and Background : Isaque Bagwan was born and raised in Mumbai,
Maharashtra. He came from a modest background and was determined to
serve in law enforcement from a young age.

Shootout at Wadala : One of the most notable events in Bagwan's career was
the "Shootout at Wadala" on January 11, 1982. Bagwan and his team
were instrumental in the operation that led to the death of gangster Manya
Surve. This encounter marked a significant moment in Mumbai's crime
history, demonstrating the police's commitment to tackling organized crime.
