' MUNIR SHEIKH '



Munir Sheikh(tushar kapoor)

- Birth and Origin : Munir Sheikh hailed from Dharavi, one of
Mumbai's largest slums, which was a breeding ground for many underworld
figures. The exact details of his birth and early life remain obscure,
typical of many gangsters of his era.

- Entry into Crime : Like many of his contemporaries, Munir was drawn
into the world of crime from a young age, initially engaging in
petty thefts and gradually moving on to more serious offenses.
Munir Sheikh was a key member of the Manya Surve gang,which was led
by the gangster Manya Surve. This gang was involved in various criminal
activities, including extortion, contract killings, and smuggling.
The Manya Surve gang was in conflict with other underworld groups,
especially those led by Dawood Ibrahim and his associates.
